Stint
— a dark-first Jira time tracker for developers · concept board

Redesign of the week tracker into a focused, keyboard-driven desktop tool. The core shift: it's no longer read-only — you log and manage hours in seconds, with a fast ticket picker, quick-duration logging, and a week shown as stacked capacity so you see exactly where you're behind. Inspired by GitButler, Linear and native macOS apps.
